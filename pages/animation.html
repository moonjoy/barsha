Chagu Bakha (One Story)

Thesis Project 2019
Completion Time: 1 month

Chagu Bakha is an animated short film about an ancient folklore about a Diety in the Newar culture in Nepal. My hope is that digital storytelling can reconnect young people from my ethnic group, the Newars, with our cultural identity. I explore digital storytelling with the memories of my hometown and my childhood within the Newari culture The animation is made entirely in Blender 2.8 with grease pencil and post production done in Adobe After Effects and Adobe Premiere.
