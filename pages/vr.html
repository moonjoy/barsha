Mythical Land

Team: Barsha and Menghe
Completion Time: 1 month

The theme of this project for us was to create something that would trigger wonder. So, my partner and I came up with this scenario where the player is the guardian of a mythical floating land which is being protected by a huge shark roaming around the space. However, the peace of this mysterious land is disrupted when the protector is attacked by lowland creatures. So, the player, as a guardian, has to destroy these lowland creatures.
